About State Life

A National Legacy. A Local Promise.

The National Foundation

State Life Insurance Corporation of Pakistan was formed in 1972 when the country's life insurance business was brought under one state-owned institution. Over five decades later, it stands as Pakistan's largest life & health insurer, carrying an AAA rating and a nationwide network that reaches communities in every province.
